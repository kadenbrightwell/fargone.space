Story

A second ship leaving Earth, bound for a new home, encounters trouble in space. A small child, awoken suddenly by a malfunction in his cryo pod, attempts to wake the others, but finds them all dead, having suffocated in their pods from a malfunction he just barely avoided. The ship is now approaching their destination but encounters more burn in the atmosphere of the encroaching planet than expected. Losing one of its engines, it crashes into the planet. And with that, we begin.

About

Far Gone is a Role-playing Game designed for Windows. While the creators of Far Gone do take inspiration from other games, Far Gone is a completely original game, and any similarities are just a coincidence (or Kaden thinking he came up with a cool idea and not realizing that a similar mechanic exists in a game he once played).

  • A unique battle system, where fighting is rendered extraneous, and all enemies can be dealt with peacefully.
  • Dodge enemy attacks and mount your own in fun and engaging battles.
  • Original art and soundtrack.
  • Captivating story with a focus on humor and connection.
  • Become friends with all of the bosses!
